About Me

Currently, I'm studying for a Ph.D. degree at the University of Texas at Dallas. My Ph.D. research is related to Mixed Reality, Augment Reality, and Virtual Reality using Microsoft HoloLens 2. Our instructor, my labmate, and I started a collaboration project with the Army Research Laboratory (ARL) team in August 2021. The research topic is about using HoloLens 2 to make applications related to Scene Understanding and measurements. Now, I have one paper under review and two other papers in writing.
My previous research areas are Machine Learning and Image Processing, and I obtained a master's degree from National Chung Cheng University. My advisor and I published a paper at workshop MUSA at ACM Multimedia 2017 and received the student travel grant from the conference.
I'm looking for a future summer internship related to Augmented Reality, Mixed Reality, Computer Vision, or Machine Learning.
